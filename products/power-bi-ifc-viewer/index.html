FAQs

Can't find an answer? Check our docs or contact support

Can I try the IFC Viewer before paying?

Yes. The Community plan lets you use the IFC Viewer in Power BI Desktop with a starter template. You only need to pay when enabling sharing in the Power BI Service.

How do I purchase Flinker Power BI Visual licenses?

You have two options:

1) Single-user licenses (self-service via Microsoft AppSource)
Buy seats in Microsoft AppSource. Billing runs through your Microsoft purchase flow, and your Microsoft 365 admin assigns the licenses to the right users.

2) Tenant-wide licenses (covers everyone, no assignments)
For tenant-wide licensing, contact us at sales@flinker.app (or book a call). Tenant-wide licensing automatically covers all users in your tenant, so no one needs to assign licenses to individuals.

Is pricing per user?

It depends on the plan. Community is free. Business is per user — each person who views or edits reports in Power BI Service needs their own license, purchased via Microsoft AppSource. Enterprise is per tenant, covering all editors and MS365 viewers in your organization with no per-user fees.

What is per-tenant pricing?

A tenant is your organization’s dedicated Microsoft 365 environment — your users, groups, domains, security policies, and SharePoint/Teams data. Enterprise is licensed per tenant, covering all editors and MS365 viewers across your organization. For project-specific licensing, embedded analytics, or broader commercial reuse, Enterprise applies.

How are Business and Enterprise billed?

Business is billed per user via Microsoft AppSource — each licensed user is assigned individually in your Microsoft 365 admin center.

Enterprise is billed per tenant. A single license covers all editors and MS365 viewers in your organization — no per-user assignments needed. For project-specific, embedded, or commercial deployment rights, talk to sales.

Do viewers also need a license?

It depends on the plan. With Business, every person who views a report in Power BI Service needs their own license. With Enterprise, all MS365 users in your tenant are covered — no per-viewer licensing needed. Community is Desktop-only, so the question does not apply.

What payment methods do you support?

Business licenses are purchased via Microsoft AppSource (Microsoft billing). For Enterprise, we offer flexible billing — invoice, PO, bank transfer, and custom arrangements. Contact sales for details.
